## Artistic Statement (philosophy)

I want to work on projects that have an impact use storytelling and emotions in an effective way.

i want to use video to give voice to important topics

Artistic expression and curiosity were always important to me.

Art and curiosity motivate me and pushed me to be the person I am now. When I was 22 I got the wild idea to turn my life around. At the time I was studying to become a Mechatronics Engineer — the one that makes robots. I started to develop an interest in films and I moved to Abu Dhabi to study Filmmaking at the New York University Abu Dhabi. This lead me to all sorts of adventures and I met some incredible people. I travelled and made many videos. Now I reside in Budapest doing the same.

Something I enjoy the most is meeting people from all over the world.

I’ve been to

-   India
-   Sri Lanka
-   Tbilisi
-   Nepal
-   Ghana
-   Shanghai

I gravitate towards humor and social issues as I made videos for The Gazelle, the NYU Abu Dhabi news outlet (naps video, cats video, Batida, 47 souls, lounge sessions, New York study away video).

In addition to film I developed an interest in interactive media to create digital experiences and to tell stories. I took part in creating a board game for inspiring students to pursue education in Nepal, an interactive musical experience and a Virtual Reality experience that premiered at the NYU Abu Dhabi Capstone Festival in 2019.

I play and perform music in three separate acts.

# Fun Fact - I was in the same movie with Brad Pitt - War Machine 2015

## I was only an extra.

...but still. This was the first professional shoot that I was part of, and it felt ... well, I'm not going to lie, _it felt pretty boring_ to wait around for our scene to start finally shooting.
