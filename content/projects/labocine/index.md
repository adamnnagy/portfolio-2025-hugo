# I was Head of Video at Labocine 2019-2021.

## My role was to

* Edit the monthly trailer for the current issue.
* Write the weekly newsletter in Mailchimp. 
* Set up Facebook ads for the monthly issues.
* Create posts and manage the company instagram account and create content for posting on Instagram https://www.instagram.com/labocine/.
* I coded a script that auto-downloaded all the videos of the current issue for easier editing workflow. This was before AI.

# Labocine

LABOCINE stands for LABOratory + CINEma. Even though it now has many identities (a platform, a magazine, a portal, an archive, a network) I mainly used it as a streaming service with monthly issues. The monthly issues adapt scientific concepts, and feature shorts, documentaries and full-length films that deal with science. They have 100,000 members and 136,000 Intagram followers.
