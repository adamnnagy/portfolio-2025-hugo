## Interactive exploration of racial biasing

I gamified the experience of unconscious biasing. Inspired by games like "Papers Please", "Undertale", "Train", the player is doing a mundane job using morally questionable biases to do well.

* In "Papers Please" you play as a border control officer. 

* In "Undertale", you can earn scores for "evil" deeds.

* In "Train", you work in train logistics, until you realize that you're orchestrating trains to Auschwitz-Birkenau.

## Why VR?

I wanted the person playing with the experience to really *feel* the identity of a goods officer. 

## Workflow

I collected data for the items online and from personal testimonials. I designed boxes so that the items are easily identifiable

# Wooden Step Sequencer

These are boxes that generate a musical synthesis by breaking the intended behavior of electric circuits. 

## The circuit

The circuit design came from the book "Handmade Electronics".

## The design 

I used wood, as it counters wires and electronics really well. The boxes generate an ominous sound, so it was apt to go for the mountains.
