## Perkupa

{{< spotify id="30GuPTkWcK5FyZOyGDjHFg">}}

## Lead Vocals and Guitar

Perkupa started from the smoldering ruins of my high school band, Brácsa. I got old members together in 2021 after realizing that my love for music has survived the Covid era. The band is still active even though our drummer lives in Copenhagen. This is an obstacle that changes the approach to how we make and release music. This turned the band into a project band, that's active for concentrated periods. Our objective is to write and publish songs and we accomplish this during our retreats to the Hungarian countryside.

So far, we have published 8 songs, performed 3 shows, and written countless songs that we are preparing to be published.

Apart from being the main songwriter, I organize logistics, I make the videos and organize concerts. I design the graphics, manage the uploads and distribution. Basically I'm the all-around guy.

Perkupa has so far successfully organized three shows:
- 2023, [Nyolcésfél](https://artkartell.hu/vizit/530-)
- 2025, [Dobódél Live Show]({{< relref "/projects/dobodel-live-perkupa/" >}})
- 2026 May, show in [Lámpás](https://www.facebook.com/lampaspub/)

## Kiel Winde

{{< spotify id="4JIGx5zBX6qEamwwqq7A9U">}}

## Lead Guitar and Background Vocals

Kiel Winde is where humanities majors meet punk infused with synths. It's more sophisticated than Viagra Boys, it's more tame than Idles, but just as loud.

I contribute to Kiel Winde as the lead guitarist and background vocalist. I joined in 2023 when Kiel Winde was already an established band. My guitar work and songwriting ideas have immediately resonated with the rest of the group. I contribute to the songwriting, sound design, melodies and guitar solos. My guitar work is featured on the most recent EP, ["A test mozog tovább"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KpvW8Fe801Y&list=OLAK5uy_nqDZQfUiz3A8son447J8ip1P7ZkG04fnE).

During my tenure with Kiel Winde, the band has grown into a prominent band of the Budapest club scene. It performs regularly in Budapest, and received recognitions at talent shows:
- NKA (National Cultural Fund) organized "Öröm a Zene" - Balassagyarmat. Special Prize, Studio time
- NKA (National Cultural Fund) organized "Öröm a Zene" - Miskolc. 2nd Place, 3 Special Prizes
- I. Lámpás Tehetségmutató - Special Prize, Studio Time with Ákos Dióssy (Kispál és a Borz)

## Juhász Zoli | from 2024

{{< spotify id="2tGfWxiNDFCunnom6Tdro4">}}

## Rhythm Guitar

I play guitar in the solo project of [Zoli Juhász](considermearockstar.com). He is an acclaimed artist in Hungary who has been around the musical scene since 2017. He released his first solo album in 2024. Zoli has won multiple awards and grants and is a defining artist of the Hungarian music scene.

Among these, the most prestigious are:
- NKA Hangfoglaló
- Kikeltető 2023

I joined the band with the studio recordings already underway for the album Csillagszemű, however I was invited to contribute to the track [Vízfolyás](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=an8OWC3r1OY). 

Playing together with the backing band of Juhász Zoli is a crazy experience. The band is put together from professional musicians who are on the top of their craft, every session I caught myself learning something new. The rehearsals are always high paced, and I am tested to the best of my abilities.

In 2024 the band was signed to do a tour of all the major festivals of Hungary. We played shows among the best bands in Hungary and met some amazing people in the backstages. On the most anticipated show of that tour, Juhász Zoli opened for Carson Coma in Veszprém, the biggest band in Hungary since 2021. I collected all the passes from that tour, as I cherish it deeply.

The pictures are from a tour that we did together.

Performing solo is relatively new for me. It all leads back to this. I started as a singer-songwriter performing open mics, and integrating this into is the place where I can use all of the skill-set I refined during my band work.

Panteézis is an important stage in that process. I'm expanding the tools that I developed during that process, integrating my songs into the live looping set.
